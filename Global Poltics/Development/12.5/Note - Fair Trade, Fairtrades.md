**Fair trade**
- equitable trading (price, etc.)
- generally between old core and peripheral
**FairTrade**
- certification on producer
- met international standards in price, premiums, labour laws, inspection and supply chain
- **Pros**
	- workers receive minimum price
	- protect against exploitation
	- FairTrade Premium is an additional sum paid to invest in social economic and environmental projects
	- promote environmentally friendly practices
		- reduction of pesticides, sustainable water use, and organic farming
	- protect labour standards
