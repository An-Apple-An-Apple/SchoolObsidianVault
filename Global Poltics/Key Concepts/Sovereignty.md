The supreme authority (power with legitimacy) of [[States]], reflected as being the sole dictator of laws within their territories.
**Internal Sovereignty**
- Supreme authority within the state.
- **Popular Sovereignty** is the sovereign that represents the will of the people
- **Absolute Sovereignty** is the sovereignty exercise by a single person
**External Sovereignty**
- Ability for [[States]] to act independently on the world stage. Territory and political independences are inviolable. No hierarchy of states.

**De Jure** sovereign is who has it in name and under the law.

**De Facto** sovereign is who have real control.
