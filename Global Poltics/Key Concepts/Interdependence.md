**Billiard Ball Model**: [[Sovereignty]] separates [[States]] into impenetrable units. They influence each other through external pressure: war and diplomacy.

**Cobweb Model**: [[States]] affairs are increasingly transnational and interdependent. Action of one have repercussions on everyone else. 

**Interdependence:** Mutual reliance between [[States]]. Can be symmetrical or asymmetrical in nature.

**Complex interdependence**: [[States]] are being forced to work together for multilateral benefits. Unilateral military actions are of less importance. 

**Global Governance**: Global order that challenges the [[Realism|realist]] notion of anarchic world order with the emergence of [[IGOs]] and [[NGOs]] that provide oversight to the whole globe. Nevertheless, these organization are but creatures of their member states.

**Globalization**: The growing [[Interdependence]] that decrease the relevance of transnational borders and physical distance. Local, national, and international events are increasingly mingled. Economic, Cultural and Political. Arguably assimilation. 

**International Society**: Relations between states are conditioned by international law and diplomacy of [[IGOs]] which maintains international order and interest.
