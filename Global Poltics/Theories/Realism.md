## Key Principals
1. Anarchy
2. People are self-fish and competitive. 
## Classical Realism
1. People want power inherently.
## Structural Realism
In an anarchic world
1. People want to survive.
2. Power is necessary of survival.
3. Competition is inevitable because of limited resource.
**Offensive Structural Realism** indicates that [[States]] should seek [[Hegemony]] in a chaotic world structure.
**Defensive Structural Realism** indicates that [[States]] should pursuit equilibrium and maintain [[peace]].
