 **Sanctions**
 - penalties or restrictive measures
 - to influence behavior, enforce norms, or punish violations
 - a [[soft power]] diplomacy tool 

**Economic Sanctions**
- restriction on trade, investments, or financial transactions
- *examples*: embargoes, asset freezes, bans on export/imports
**Diplomatic Sanctions**
- reduction or suspension of diplomatic ties
- *examples*: withdrawal of ambassadors, cancellation os official visits
**Military Sanctions**
- arms embargoes or restrictions on military cooperation
- *examples*: banning sale of weapons or military technology
**Travel Sanctions**
- bans on travel for specific individuals
- examples: visa restrictions for political leaders or elites, (including taking children so parents return)
**Targeted (Smart) Sanctions**
- directed at specific individuals, companies, or sectors
- examples: freezing assets of oligarchs or banning transactions with certain firms
**Multilateral vs Unilateral Sanctions**
- **Multilateral**: imposed by the international bodies like the UN or EU
- **Unilateral**: imposed by a single country, such as the US or China
