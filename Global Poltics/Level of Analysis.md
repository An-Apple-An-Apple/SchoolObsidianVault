The levels of impact of a certain issue.

# Local
- Impact inhabitants of a specific local area.
- Ie. Fire in Southwestern Ontario

# National
- Impact strictly bounded to geographic boundary of nation-states.
- Ie. US federal election

# Regional
- Impact across border but not encompassing multiple whole nations.
- Ie. Fishing Industry of the Great Lakes

# International
- Impact on multiple nation-states, but not the whole world
- Ie. Canadian-US trade

# Global
- Impact is longterm to everyone.
- Ie. Climate Change
