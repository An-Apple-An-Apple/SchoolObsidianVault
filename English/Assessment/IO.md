## Global Issue
1. **Depth**: clear importance and relevant
2. **An embedded argument**: an [argument] should be within this global issue
3. **A cause and effect relationship**: pick a issue then find a [cause]
4. **Complex but not convoluted**: clear and easy to digest, to big -> to messy
5. **A repeatable phrase**: one phrase that is an [anchor] to the global issue

# Step 1. Picking Global Issue.
1. Pick one of the [[General#Fields of Inquiry]]
2. Ask "W" questions
	1. Which specific Field of Inquiry matches best to this text?
	2. What makes you say that?
	3. What are the  key conflicts of the work and how do they match to the field of inquiry?
	4. Which characters capture your idea best?
	5. What moments in the text really illustrate your GI?
	6. Why is that moment critical to understand the GI?
	7. What features does the author use to accentuate the GI?
3. Create a topic.
	1. ie. **Social Media**
4. Create a refined topic
	1. ie. **The effect of social media on self esteem of teenager**
5. Create a global issue
	1. ie. **Today I’d like to discuss the Field of Inquiry of Culture, identity & community. More specifically, I aim to investigate how psychological mechanisms of socia media causes deceit of self esteem of teenager.**
